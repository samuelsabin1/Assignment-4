When Hunger Strikes

Our Savior

How many times have you been either sitting at your desk or climbing down a pole or even four hours into your route that you feel a sharp and a thunder crash in your loins? If you are like me no doubt you crouch in fear of the inevitable of whats to come. Yes sirr, you hear it comming DUH DUH DUH DUHHHHH!!!!! "(insert roaring lion sound clip)" yes you hear it that roar, the sound of your people, you ancestors have felt it you gramps has felt it. Then you long for the days of your savior, GRANDMA!.

Wonderful is it this old lady she knows the inner works and is a master at taming and appeasing the beast known as hunger. Who else can compare to the thankgiving dinners, the fried chicken, the mac n cheese with the perfectly crispen cheese thats stuck to the side of the pan. The rice and beans with legume where every spoonful contains the perfect ratio of rice, bean sauce, legume, and beef (or pork depending on what is avaible). This God given human was an absolute blessing to your youth. You knew when hunger strikes at grandma's house you will be fed and fed you will and hunger shall be no more.

Come with me on this short mini adventure where you come to know the 3 best parts of the day.
